About Me

Hello! I'm Felicia, a passionate crochet artist with a love for creating timeless pieces out of wool. My crochet journey began when I was in Grade 5, when my mom first taught me how to crochet. What started as a fun skill she passed down to me quickly grew into a lifelong passion.

I discovered the joy of turning a simple ball of yarn into something beautiful, whether it was my first scarf, a cozy hat, or now, the colorful ruffles, scrunchies, and tops I design today. Crocheting became more than just a craft; it became a way to express creativity, patience, and love in every stitch.

Each of my handmade pieces is created with care and attention to detail, inspired by my journey and the lessons my mom taught me. I believe handmade items carry a story, and my goal is to share that warmth and creativity with everyone who wears my creations.
